Sam Kerr

Sam Kerr is an Australian footballer, known as the captain of the Matildas(A women's team). She is the all - time leading scorer of the Matildas and plays for chelsea FC.

A little bit about her.

Sam Kerr comes from a large, athletic family in Western Australia, with her father, Roger, and older brother, Daniel, both being professional Australian rules footballers. She also has a deep South Asian heritage through her paternal grandfather, Denzil Kerr, who was an Indian boxer, and her paternal grandmother, Coral Kerr, who played competitive basketball.
